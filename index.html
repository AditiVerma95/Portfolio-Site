Hey! I'm Aditi Verma, an aspiring Unity developer focused on 2D/3D gameplay systems and interactive UI. I love exploring what makes games feel good — from tight controls to satisfying feedback loops.

I've worked on mobile games, VR tools, and solo projects ranging from tower defense systems to gravity-switching platformers. Currently a B.Tech CS student at Amity University Rajasthan (2023–2027).

Experienced with Unity, C#, Blender, Photoshop, and Aseprite. Passionate about building polished, responsive game experiences.
